Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Digitrade AS collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR.

Last updated: March 18, 2026

What is Personal Information?

Personal information is any information that can be used to identify you as an individual, such as name, email address, phone number, IP address, or other contact details.

Collection of Information

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Contact us through forms or email
  • Sign up for newsletters or demonstrations
  • Use our services

We may also automatically collect technical information, such as IP address, browser type, and usage statistics.

Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and questions
  • Deliver and improve our services
  • Send relevant information about our products
  • Comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: These are necessary for the website to function, such as remembering language preferences and theme settings.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the website, for example, Google Analytics.
  • Functional cookies: Store preferences like theme (light/dark mode).

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except:

  • When necessary to provide services (e.g., hosting partners)
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • In connection with the sale or merger of the company

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Erase information (right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us via our contact page.

Security

We take security seriously and use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. This includes encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

Retention of Information

We retain your information for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. We regularly review information to ensure it is not retained longer than necessary.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the website or email. The last update date is indicated at the top of the document.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us.
