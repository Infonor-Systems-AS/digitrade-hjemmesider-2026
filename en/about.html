About us

About Infonor Systems

Infonor Systems is a Norwegian technology company that develops and delivers solutions for efficient, data-driven B2B commerce. Since our founding in 1998, our goal has been clear: to make complex trade and information processes simpler, more precise, and more profitable.

We combine solid technological expertise with deep understanding of business processes in value chains where many actors, systems, and data sources must interact seamlessly.

Who we are

Infonor Systems is an IT competence company with extensive experience in electronic commerce, system integration, and decision support.

We work closely with our customers and always start from their existing systems, processes, and needs.

Our strength lies in the ability to unite technology and business – and turn data into practical value.

What we do

We develop and deliver solutions that provide better control, better decisions, and increased profitability throughout the value chain. Our solutions collect and compile relevant data from the company's systems, so that purchasing, assortment, and sales processes can be managed on a fact-based foundation.

Infonor Systems has particular expertise in:

  • Electronic commerce (B2B)
  • Integration with ERP and specialist systems
  • Data collection, structuring, and analysis
  • Business-critical and scalable IT solutions

Our products

Infonor Systems is behind the proprietary solutions Digitrade and Digitrade Presale – advanced tools for sales, purchasing, and decision support.

The solutions are developed in close collaboration with customers in trade and distribution, and are used by well-known players in the sports and retail sectors. What these customers have in common is the need for better collaboration, reduced risk, and higher profitability.

How we work

We believe in long-term customer relationships and pragmatic solutions that actually work in practice.

Our working methodology is based on:

  • Close dialogue and strong business understanding
  • Effective use of existing systems
  • Structured execution and high quality in deliveries

For us, technology is not about technology alone – but about creating measurable value for the customer.

Our role

Infonor Systems is a partner for companies seeking better flow, better insight, and better decision support in their trade and information processes.

We help our customers realize the potential in the data they already have.
